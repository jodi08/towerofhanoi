Tower of Hanoi

Rules:

  • You can only move one disk at a time
  • Each move will take the top disk from the first tower clicked on and place it on the second tower clicked on
  • You can only place a disk on top of a bigger disk
  • The goal is to move all the disks from the first tower to the last tower
  • You WIN when all four disks are on the last tower from small to large
